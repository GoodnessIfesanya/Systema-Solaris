Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and is the fifth planet out from the Sun. It is two and a half times more massive than all the other planets in the solar system combined. It is made primarily of gases and is therefore known as a “gas giant”.Jupiter has the shortest day of all the planets. It turns on its axis once every 9 hours and 55 minutes.
